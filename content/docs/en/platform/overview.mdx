# Commet Billing Platform

Commet is the billing infrastructure for SaaS companies that need to scale beyond basic subscriptions. Built for LATAM businesses, Commet handles complex pricing models, usage-based billing, and enterprise contracts with the flexibility to adapt to your business model.

<Callout type="info">
Platform documentation aims at business operators. For SDKs, endpoints, and code samples head to the Developer Library.
</Callout>

## Why Commet?

Modern SaaS companies need billing infrastructure that goes beyond simple monthly subscriptions. Whether you're launching with freemium pricing, negotiating enterprise deals, or experimenting with usage-based models, your billing system should enable growth—not limit it.

Commet provides:

- **Flexible pricing models**: From simple subscriptions to complex usage-based pricing with tiers, credits, seats, and hybrid models
- **Enterprise-ready**: Support for custom contracts, negotiated pricing, and approval workflows
- **LATAM compliance**: Built-in support for local tax regulations, currencies, and invoicing requirements
- **Developer-first**: Comprehensive APIs and SDKs to integrate billing into your product experience

## Key Capabilities

### Product Catalog & Pricing
Define your products and pricing strategies with support for 9+ pricing models including fixed subscriptions, usage-based, tiered pricing, seat-based, and hybrid combinations.

[Learn about Product Catalog →](../products/catalog-overview)

### Agreements
Create and manage customer contracts with custom pricing, discounts, and terms. Handle everything from self-serve subscriptions to enterprise negotiations with built-in approval workflows.


### Usage Tracking & Metering
Ingest and process usage events in real-time. Track API calls, compute hours, storage, transactions—any metric that drives your pricing.

[Learn about Usage Metrics →](../products/usage-based-pricing)

### Billing & Invoicing
Automated billing cycles with support for prepaid, postpaid, and hybrid models. Generate compliant local invoices with proper tax handling for LATAM markets.
